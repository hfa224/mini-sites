Fine, short uncle holding in laugh in Madeira (7)

Give me a hint!

The answer is a place "in Madeira" and begins with F.

I give up, tell me the answer

FUNCHAL; definition: in Madeira

F (Fine, abbrev) + "uncl" (short uncle) around (holding) laugh="ha"

Short, backwards low-carb diet one is stuck in - but it mixes ice cream and beer?! (7)

Give me a hint!

This is a drink that mixes ice cream and beer... What is the most famous low carb diet?

I give up, tell me the answer

NIKITA; definition: mixes ice cream and beer

ATKINS="low carb diet" made short "ATKIN" and reversed ("backwards") NIKTA with i ("one is stuck in") = NIKITA
